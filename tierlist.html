Best to Worst Mini trucks

Out of the late 80s to early 90s options

  1. Mazda B-series

    2. Yes, I am biased. They have by far the best extended cab, and even though they have the weakest engines (2.0 fe/2.2 f2/ 2.6 g6), the f family of engines came in 12 valve turbo variants in the 626/probe GTs. my favorite styling, amazing build quality, reasonable prices, and tons of aftermarket

  2. Mitsubishi Mighty Max/ Dodge D50

    3. Great styling, and all of the provisions to swap in a 4g63. Less aftermarket than most of the other options, but the grille on these mini's single handly carries it to 2nd place.

  3. Izusu P'up

    4. I just like these a lot. My roomate has one and its always breaking, but we get it back running easily. Bonus points for the joe izusu ads

  4. Nissan d21 Hardbody

    5. Out of all of the japanese made mini trucks, I think these aged the worst. They are on the more expensive side of things, and I dont fit very well in the cab. The saving grace is of course the engine, a KA24e. AKA, the 240sx engine (techincally thats a KA24de, but close enough). Which makes for the most powerful 4 cillinder engine but in a mini truck from this time.

  5. Chevrolet S10

    6. Cheaply made and ugly as hell, buuuttttt... a 4.3 v6 with a 5 speed in a single cab s10 is the most power per pound you can get in a mini truck stock. Burnouts for days.

  6. Toyota Pickup

    7. Yeah yeah toyota reliability. They look good, the 22re in them is iconic, and it was in back to the future. Why is it down this far? Two words, Toyota Tax. A 270,000 B-series with a blown engine but straight body is 500$. The same condition Toyota is 5k. (only a slight exaguration)

  7. Ford Ranger

    8. They are everywhere, just driving till they fall apart. Extended cabs are hideous in my opinion, but they are the absolute cheapest entry point. supply and demand I guess

  8. Jeep Commanche

    9. Is this a mini truck? Kinda. I was convinced to add this to the list, but im not placing it highly out of spite.

  9. Dodge dakota

    10. These are not mini trucks. I hate these, genuinely. They look like a drunk man designed them with a ruler and couldnt keep his pen straight. This one is on the list purely so I can hate on it.
