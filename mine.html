My '91 B series

This is my B series, it is the second mini truck ive owned, and by far my favorite vehicle i've owned ( I'm a habitual facebook marketplace flipper). I have completely rebuilt the top half of the engine, added a weber dgev carb, long tube headers, and a 2.5 exhaust with a flowmaster 40 series. Suspension wise it is lowered 4 inches at the current moment (I am constantly lowering it more and more), with beltech front and rear sway bars added. All this work makes for a wicked fun 70 hp ripper. Interior wise Ive sourced OEM buckets, and pieced together a full sound system with a 500w 10" sub, 2 100w 6" speakers, and 2 50w 4"s. The wheels are from a 2019 Tacoma trd. Front end wise I've added 7 inch halos from a jeep by drilling and cutting away the grille until they fit, and added a chrome bumper bar. It is loud, slow, low, and stupid as hell, just as a minitruck should be
